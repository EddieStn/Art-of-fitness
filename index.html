Benefits of Fitness:


  • Mental health (relieve stress, help with anxiety and depression)
  • Muscle gain
  • Toning of body
  • Fat loss
  • Form good posture

Important tips:


  • Warm up before every workout (exercises that gets your blood running (jumping jacks) not static movements)
  • Stretch after every workout (static exercises)
  • Perform your exercises in good form to avoid injuries
  • Give your body time to rest(allow at least a day between workouts of the same muscle so that your muscle fibres can regenerate and grow)
