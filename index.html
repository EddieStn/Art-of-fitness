Important tips:


  • Warm up prior

    Exercises that gets your blood running (jumping jacks, knee taps), not static movements.

  • Good form

    Perform your exercises in good form to avoid injuries (keep your back straight when bending for anything).

  • Hit the muscle

    Perform the exercises as they are instructed for maximum gains, otherwise your efforts may target a different muscle.

  • Stretch after

    Do static exercises, specially for the muscles you were working.

  • Rest

    Give your body time to rest(allow at least a day between workouts of the same muscle so that your muscle fibres can regenerate and grow).
